USER HELPER FOR E-VOTING PLUGIN

How to add E-Voting Activity

" NOTE ' You Must Login As a teacher ' "

1) Click on login

2) Enter your user name and your password and then Click 'login'

3) Click on Site Home

4) Click on Current Course

6) Click on turn editing on

7) Choose the Annonces that you want and Click 'Add an activity or resource '

8) Select on E-Voting activity

9) Click on 'Add'



How to create a new E-Voting poll

1) Click on Questions

2) Write your Question

3) add choices

4) Click on 'Add Question'


As Student Enter To enter Voting On Button

1) click on QR Code

2) enter Your URl as Student

3)Enter Start button

4)Select Time for voting

5)Enter on any option You want it

6)enter stop


IF You want to reset Your option

1) click on button Reset

2) Enter Option Again

3) Enter Stop
